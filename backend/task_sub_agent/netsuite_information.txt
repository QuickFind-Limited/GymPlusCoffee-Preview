Wholesale Customer: Payment Terms = 2/3/other
Retail Customer: Payment Terms = NULL 

the specific payment terms field name in NetSuite is:

  terms

  This field contains:
  - NULL/empty = No payment terms (retail customers, immediate payment)
  - 2 = NET 30 days payment terms
  - 3 = NET 60 days payment terms
  - Other numeric IDs for custom payment terms

  You can query it directly as:
  SELECT terms FROM customer

  The field references the payment term ID from the paymentterm table, where you can look up the
  actual term names if needed.
